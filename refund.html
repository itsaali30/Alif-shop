Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated on 28-12-2025

MOHD RAFIQ ALI believes in helping its customers as far as possible and has a liberal cancellation policy.

  • Cancellations will be considered only if requested immediately after placing the order.
  • Cancellation may not be entertained if orders have already been communicated to vendors.
  • Cancellation is not accepted for perishable items like flowers and eatables.
  • Damaged or defective items must be reported within 7 days of receipt.
  • Refunds approved by MOHD RAFIQ ALI will take 9-15 days to process.
